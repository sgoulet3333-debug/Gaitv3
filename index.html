ACL Walk Gait Analyzer v5

Upload a side-view walking video. The app estimates knee motion, step timing, trunk compensation, and side-to-side differences.

1. Record correctly

  • Film 8–12 natural steps from the side.
  • Keep the full body visible and phone still at hip height.
  • Use a level walkway, fitted clothing, and good lighting.
  • Short clips under 30 seconds work best on a phone.

2. Choose and analyze

Your video is not uploaded to a server.

3. Screening results

Suggestions to discuss with your PT

    Important limits

    This is an educational tracking tool, not a diagnosis or medical device. A single phone video cannot measure graft integrity, joint forces, or true 3D gait. Do not change your rehabilitation plan based only on this app.

    Stop and contact a clinician for worsening swelling, instability, locking, wound drainage, calf swelling, chest pain, or shortness of breath.
